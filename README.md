## Overview

Forge is a product studio that designs and develops custom operational software for growth-focused organizations. Every system is built with production durability and client ownership in mind — once delivered, you own the application, the data, and the infrastructure.

## Capabilities

### CRM Extensions & Integrations
Tools that connect, enrich, and automate CRM operations, turning your CRM into the operational hub it was meant to be.

### Operational Dashboards & Internal Apps
Real-time UI for business operations — owner-controlled and maintainable — so teams can see what matters and make decisions faster.

### Workflow Automation Engines
Systems that eliminate manual work and sync across platforms, freeing teams to focus on strategy instead of repetitive tasks.

### Event & Check-In Systems
Lightweight operational tooling built for live experiences, providing seamless operations at scale.

## How It Works

1. **Define the Outcome** — Align on the operational problem and the measurable result the system must deliver.
2. **Architect & Build** — Design and develop a production-ready application tied to that outcome.
3. **Deploy & Transfer** — Deploy the system into your environment. You own the application, data, and infrastructure. Post-launch support is available.

## Systems in Practice

Forge builds operational infrastructure that supports the broader Sandbox ecosystem and client growth initiatives. Examples include:

- **GTM Infrastructure for Sandbox-GTM** — Internal tooling and workflow automation systems that power campaign orchestration, data synchronization, and operational visibility.
- **Event Operations Platforms** — Custom-built check-in systems, attendee lookup tools, and CRM extensions designed for real-time performance in live environments.
- **Workflow Automation Layers** — Lightweight applications that eliminate manual processes and create reliable data flow across marketing and sales systems.
